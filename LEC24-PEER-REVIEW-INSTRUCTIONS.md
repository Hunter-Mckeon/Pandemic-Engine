## What to Do

1. **Your partner team will share their submission with you during class.** The
   instructor will display both teams' submissions side-by-side. You do not need
   access to their GitHub repo — we will project or distribute the content directly.

2. Read your partner's submission as a team (~15 min). Focus on:

   - **Their database choice** — how does it differ from yours, and what does that mean
     for how each system handles queries or scale?

   - **Their AI comparison** — what did Copilot get right or wrong about their system?
     Is that similar to or different from what Copilot said about yours?

   - **Something they have that you don't** — a design decision, analytic, or Phase 3
     feature that your system is missing. Is it something you'd want?

3. Discuss as a team, then fill out the Gradescope submission together.

## Deliverable

One Gradescope submission per team with your answers to the three reflection questions
above. See the Gradescope assignment for the full rubric.
